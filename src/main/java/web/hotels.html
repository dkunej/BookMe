Hotel Solaire ★★

This economy class hotel, situated near the convention center, is located within minutes of the Staples Center. Provides patrons with an ideal area in which to explore the business and entertainment centers of the LA area, Beverly Hills, Venice Beach, Universal Studios LA, and Hollywood. Guests will have easy access to major corporations, shopping venues, beaches, and fine restaurants. The southern California Beaches, China Town, Dodger Stadium, and Universal City Walk are nearby. The Mediterranean-styled rooms feature pastel colors, maritime themes, and intricately constructed furniture. Perfect for travelers that want a location close to a myriad of tourist attractions.

New York

Row NYC Hotel ★★★

The Row NYC hotel has pioneered a new era of individuality among Times Square hotels by meeting NYC’s signature urban grit with grandeur. Row NYC transforms your stay into a completely contemporary experience – with a front row view to Times Square that’s truly incomparable. Outside the hotel, guests have access to anything and everything Times Square has to offer – 24/7. Inside, the Row NYC is a medley of inspired New York City amenities.

Paris

Paris Hotel ★★★★★

The Hotel du Louvre is a Parisian luxury hotel of the second empire style, 5 stars category. It is located opposite the Louvre Museum, Place André-Malraux in the 1st arrondissement. It is owned by the Constellation Hotels Holdings Group and operated by the Hyatt chain.
